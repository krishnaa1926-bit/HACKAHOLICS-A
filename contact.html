Common Questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent technical issues, we aim to respond within 4-6 hours.

Can I visit your office for support?

Yes, you can visit our office for in-person consultations. We recommend scheduling an appointment in advance to ensure our team is available to assist you.

Do you offer phone support?

Yes, we provide phone support during business hours (Monday-Friday, 9 AM-6 PM IST). For urgent issues outside business hours, please use our emergency support line.

How can I partner with Hackaholics?

We're always interested in partnering with companies, educational institutions, and organizations. Please select "Partnership Opportunities" as your subject and provide details about your organization.

What information should I include in my message?

Please include your full name, contact information, and a detailed description of your inquiry. If you're reporting a technical issue, please include your browser information and steps to reproduce the problem.
